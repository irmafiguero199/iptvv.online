Is Cable Better Than IPTV for Anything?

Cable has three genuine advantages: (1) It works independently of your internet connection — if your internet goes down, cable still works. (2) It's available in rural areas where internet speed is limited (below 25 Mbps). (3) Older viewers who aren't comfortable with apps may prefer the familiarity of a cable remote and box.

For anyone with reliable broadband internet (25+ Mbps) and basic comfort with a Firestick or Smart TV, IPTV is objectively better value in every way.
