📌 Quick Answer — Smart TV IPTV Setup

Samsung Smart TV: Install "Smart IPTV" or "IPTV Smarters" from Smart Hub → Enter credentials. LG Smart TV: Install "Smart IPTV" from LG Content Store. Android TV (Sony/TCL/Philips): Install TiviMate from Google Play Store. All take under 10 minutes with iptvv.online credentials.

Setup Guide: Samsung Smart TV

1
Open Samsung Smart Hub
Press the Home button on your Samsung remote → Navigate to the Apps section → Click the Search icon (magnifying glass).
2
Search for IPTV App
Type "Smart IPTV" or "IPTV Smarters" → Install the app (free from Samsung App Store).
3
Note Your MAC Address
Open Smart IPTV → The app shows your TV's MAC address on screen. You'll need this for activation.
4
Activate with iptvv.online
Contact us on WhatsApp with your MAC address → We send you the activation URL → Enter it in Smart IPTV → All 25,000+ channels load instantly.

Setup Guide: LG Smart TV (WebOS)

1
Open LG Content Store
Press the Home button → Select LG Content Store → Go to Apps section.
2
Install Smart IPTV
Search "Smart IPTV" → Install (free). Note: Some LG models support IPTV Smarters — search for both.
3
Enter Your Credentials
Open the app → Select "Add Playlist" → Choose "Xtream Codes API" → Enter your iptvv.online server URL, username, and password from your welcome email.
4
Watch!
Channel list loads automatically. Browse by category — Canadian, Sports, French, International, Movies & Series.

Setup Guide: Android TV (Sony, TCL, Philips)

Android TV gives you access to the Google Play Store, which means you can install TiviMate — the best IPTV app available. Install TiviMate from the Play Store → Open → Add playlist → Xtream Codes API → Enter your iptvv.online credentials. Done in 3 minutes.

What is the best IPTV app for Samsung Smart TV in Canada?+
Smart IPTV is the most popular IPTV app for Samsung Tizen Smart TVs in Canada. It requires a one-time $5.99 activation fee after a 7-day free trial, and supports all Samsung models from 2014 onwards. IPTV Smarters Pro is a free alternative that works on both Samsung and LG TVs. Both work perfectly with iptvv.online IPTV credentials.
Can I use IPTV on my LG Smart TV in Canada?+
Yes. LG WebOS Smart TVs support Smart IPTV (available from the LG Content Store) and IPTV Smarters. LG TVs from 2015 onwards are compatible. Simply install Smart IPTV, enter your iptvv.online M3U URL or Xtream Codes credentials, and your full channel list loads automatically.
How many screens can I watch simultaneously on my Smart TV?+
iptvv.online plans support 1-4 simultaneous streams depending on your plan. Monthly = 1 screen, 3-month = 2 screens, 6-month = 3 screens, Yearly = 4 screens. This means your Smart TV in the living room, a Firestick in the bedroom, and a phone — all streaming different channels at the same time with no slowdown.
