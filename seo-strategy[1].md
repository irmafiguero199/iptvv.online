## 🔗 INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY

### Hub → Spoke Pattern
- Every hub page links to ALL its spokes
- Every spoke page links back to its hub + homepage
- Anchor text: Use exact match or partial match keywords
- Example: Hub /iptv-ontario/ links to /iptv-toronto/ with anchor "IPTV Toronto"

### Cross-Silo Links
- Device guides → Province pages ("Set up IPTV Firestick in Ontario")
- Province pages → Legal page ("Is IPTV legal in Quebec?")
- All pages → /pricing/ and /free-trial/

### Anchor Text Distribution (per page)
- 40% — Exact match (e.g., "IPTV Ontario")
- 30% — Partial match (e.g., "IPTV service in Ontario")
- 20% — Branded (e.g., "iptvv.online IPTV")
- 10% — Natural ("click here", "learn more", "find out")
