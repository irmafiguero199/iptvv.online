IPTV Smarters Troubleshooting Canada

  • Channels not loading: Check your internet connection. Restart the app. Verify your credentials are entered correctly (no spaces).
  • Buffering on TSN: Lower stream quality to 720p temporarily. Usually caused by slow internet speed (need 10+ Mbps for HD).
  • App crashes: Update to latest version. Clear app cache on Android.
  • Can't find TSN or RDS: Go to Live TV → Categories → Canadian Sports. Or use the search function within the app.
