How to Install IPTV on Samsung TV Canada

1
Open Samsung App Store
Press the Home button on your Samsung remote, navigate to Apps, then click the Search (magnifying glass) icon in the top right.
2
Search "Smart IPTV"
Type "Smart IPTV" (or "SS IPTV" for the free option) and press Enter. Select the app from the results.
3
Install and Open the App
Click Install, wait for the download to complete, then select Open. Note the MAC address shown on the screen (needed for Smart IPTV activation).
4
Enter iptvv.online M3U URL or Xtream Codes
For Smart IPTV: visit siptv.eu on your phone/computer and enter your TV's MAC address and iptvv.online M3U URL. For SS IPTV or Smarters: enter Xtream Codes (server URL, username, password) directly in the app.
5
Channels Load in 60 Seconds
Restart the app on your TV. All 25,000+ channels including TSN 1-5, RDS, CBC, and Sportsnet appear within 60 seconds. Find Canadian Sports in the category menu.
