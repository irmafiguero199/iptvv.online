What is an M3U Playlist?

M3U (MPEG URL) is the standard format for IPTV channel lists. When you subscribe to iptvv.online IPTV, you receive three credentials: a server URL, a username, and a password. These can be entered as an M3U URL in any compatible IPTV player app. The M3U file contains all your channel links, organized by category — Canadian channels, sports, French channels, international, VOD, and more.

How to Set Up M3U on Firestick

Here is the step-by-step process to set up your iptvv.online M3U playlist on an Amazon Firestick.

  • Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → Enable Apps from Unknown Sources
  • Install Downloader from the Amazon App Store
  • Use Downloader to install IPTV Smarters Pro or TiviMate
  • Open the app and select 'Add Playlist' or 'Xtream Codes API'
  • Enter your iptvv.online server URL, username, and password (from your welcome email)
  • Save and wait for your channel list to load — usually under 30 seconds
  • Browse 25,000+ channels organized by country, sport, genre, and language
